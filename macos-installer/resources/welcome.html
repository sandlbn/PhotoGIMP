Welcome to PhotoGIMP

PhotoGIMP transforms GIMP into a layout familiar to Adobe Photoshop users.

What will be installed:

  • Photoshop-like keyboard shortcuts
  • Reorganized tool layout
  • Optimized window & panel positions
  • Custom splash screen
  • Pre-defined canvas templates
Note: Your existing GIMP config will be backed up automatically.
Please close GIMP before installing.
